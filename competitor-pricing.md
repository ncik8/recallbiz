# Competitor Pricing — recallbiz research

Researched: 2026-06-17 (Wed). For Sunday 2026-06-21 sketch.

## Direct business card scanner competitors

### CamCard (Intsig, Chinese, oldest player 2009)
- **Personal free:** ~10 cards scanned free
- **Business Premium:** $21/user/month (min 10 users = $210/mo)
- **Business Professional:** $25/user/month (min 10 users = $250/mo)
- **10-day business free trial**
- AI email writing, tags, smart categorization, CRM sync (Salesforce/Dynamics/Google/Outlook)
- **Weakness:** No Telegram integration. No M3/NLU. China data residency concerns for some users.
- Source: camcard.com/pricing

### Eight (Sansan, Japanese)
- **Free tier:** unlimited card scanning (basic features)
- **Eight Premium:** ¥600/month (~ $4/mo) or ¥6,000/year (~ $40/yr)
- Premium adds: digitize both card sides, find common connections, contact list integration
- **Weakness:** Japan-centric, no Telegram-native UX, no M3 features
- Source: 8card.net/en

### Blinq (Australia)
- **Free:** 2 cards, unlimited sharing, no branding
- **Premium:** $7.33/mo (annual) for 5 cards
- **Business:** $4.99/user/month (annual, 5-card min)
- NFC cards start at $14 (vs V1CE $611 luxury)
- **Weakness:** Mostly digital card sharing, weaker on OCR for received paper cards
- Source: blinq.me/blog/comparing-costs

### Popl / V1CE / Wave (digital card leaders)
- More on "share my card" side than "scan their card"
- Premium $10-20/mo range
- Less relevant for recallbiz (we're scanning what we receive)

## CRM / contact management competitors

### folk CRM (relationship-focused)
- **Starter:** $10/user/month ($8 annual) — 2,000 contacts
- **Pro:** $20/user/month ($16 annual) — unlimited + AI (folkX)
- **Enterprise:** custom
- (Note: lightfield reports newer pricing $30-60/user/mo after enterprise push)
- **Weakness:** Generic CRM, no Telegram integration, no event context
- Source: folk.com / linktly.com

### Clay (data workflow builder)
- **Free:** 100 Data Credits/month
- **Launch:** $167-185/month
- **Growth:** $446-495/month
- **Enterprise:** ~$30K+/year average
- New pricing March 2026 split into Data Credits + Actions
- **Weakness:** Sales/GTM tooling, way overkill for personal contact management
- Source: clay.com

### HubSpot CRM
- Free tier (basic, up to 1M contacts)
- Starter: $20/mo per user (paid marketing features)
- Pro: $890/mo (3 users, full marketing)
- **Weakness:** Marketing/CRM focus, no Telegram, no event context
- Source: hubspot.com

## Personal CRMs

### Dex
- ~$4.99/month (iOS/macOS)
- Personal CRM with reminders, contact history
- **Weakness:** No Telegram, no OCR

### Monica
- Free tier (limited)
- Premium: $4.99/mo or $49/yr
- Personal CRM, contacts + reminders + gifts
- **Weakness:** Self-hosted option exists but no Telegram integration
- **Note:** Has both personal and team editions

## recallbiz positioning (proposed)
| | Free | Pro | Team |
|---|---|---|---|
| **recallbiz** | **10 cards** | **$9.99/mo or $99/yr** | **$49/mo** |
| CamCard | 10 (then pay) | $21-25/mo (10 user min) | — |
| Eight | unlimited basic | $4/mo (¥600) | — |
| folk CRM | none | $20/mo | — |
| Clay | 100 credits | $167+/mo | — |

**recallbiz is 2-10x cheaper than incumbents with the Telegram-native + M3 differentiators.**

Key differentiators vs. all competitors:
1. **Only Telegram-native.** No install for recipients of shared contact cards.
2. **Only one with event context** (trip mode + deep-link events).
3. **Only one with M3 follow-up drafts** built into core product.
4. **Cheapest path to unlimited cards** with Pro features ($9.99 vs $20+ for folk).
5. **Only one with native vCard share back via Telegram** = viral loop.
