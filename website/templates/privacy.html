Last updated: June 20, 2026 · TRCE (trce.io)

TRCE is a contact memory service. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. We've written it in plain English on purpose.

2. Data collected automatically

  • Server logs — Railway logs IP addresses, request paths, and response codes for 7 days. Used for debugging and abuse detection.
  • Cookies — we set one session cookie when you log in to the dashboard. No tracking cookies, no third-party analytics, no ad pixels.

3. How we use your data

We use your data only to operate TRCE. Specifically:

  • To authenticate you and keep you logged in.
  • To extract structured contact data from business card photos you send.
  • To send transactional emails (magic links, payment receipts, password resets).
  • To respond to your support requests.

We do not:

  • Sell your data to anyone, ever.
  • Share your data with advertisers or data brokers.
  • Train external AI models on your contacts or card photos.
  • Use your contacts to build any kind of social graph.

5. AI processing

When you forward a business card photo or screenshot to the TRCE Telegram bot, the image is sent to our AI provider (a large language model) to extract structured contact fields (name, email, phone, etc). The extracted text is saved; the image itself is not retained.

Our AI provider does not train on your inputs and does not retain them after the request completes, per their API terms.

6. Your rights

You can at any time:

  • Access — download all your contacts as CSV from the dashboard.
  • Edit — change any contact field from the dashboard.
  • Delete — remove individual contacts, or delete your whole account (email hello@contact.trce.io).
  • Export — get a machine-readable copy of everything we hold on you (email us).

7. Data retention after account deletion

When you request account deletion:

  • Your contacts, profile, and bot history are permanently deleted within 30 days.
  • Anonymized aggregate metrics (e.g. "user X had 50 contacts") may be retained for analytics.
  • Stripe retains payment records for 7 years per tax law — but only your customer ID, not your contacts.

8. Security

We protect your data with:

  • HTTPS everywhere (TLS 1.3).
  • bcrypt-hashed passwords (cost factor 12).
  • Row-level security on the database — even staff queries go through the app layer.
  • Service-role keys never exposed to the browser.

No system is 100% secure. If we discover a breach affecting your data, we'll email you within 72 hours.

9. Children's data

TRCE is not for users under 16. We don't knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has created an account, email us and we'll delete it.

10. International transfers

TRCE is operated from Hong Kong. Your data may be processed in the US (Railway, Stripe, Resend) or Singapore (Supabase). By using TRCE, you consent to these transfers.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We'll email you at least 14 days before material changes take effect.

12. Contact

Privacy questions? Email hello@contact.trce.io or use the contact form.
