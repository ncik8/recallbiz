How it works

Three steps.
One Telegram chat.

No app to download. No new account to create. If you can message a friend on Telegram, you can use trce.

01

Save any way

Forward a Telegram QR image. Snap a paper business card. Type /save. The bot captures it and asks for context — what you talked about, what's the next step, anything personal.

02

Ask AI, not the search bar

Skip keyword guessing. Type "do I have any investors from Sequoia" or "who did I meet last week that I haven't followed up with". The bot interprets and answers in natural language.

03

Get drafts that sound like you

Type /followup Jane. The AI uses your notes + the conversation context + their role to draft a 3-sentence follow-up that references what you actually talked about. No more cold outreach.

AI-native

Ask your network.
In plain English.

No SQL. No keyword guessing. No dropdown filters. Just ask the way you'd ask a colleague.

⌕

Natural-language search

Find anyone across your entire network by asking in plain English. Filters by company, role, tag, event, warmth — all interpreted automatically.

You: do I have any investors in my list
Bot: 6 investors saved. Top matches: @polychain_jane (Token2049), @sequoia_k (afterparty), @animoca_alice (panel)…
✎

Follow-up drafts in your voice

One command per draft. The AI uses your conversation notes, the contact's role, and your last interaction to write a follow-up that doesn't feel cold.

You: /followup Jane
Bot: "Hi Jane — great meeting at the afterparty. Sent the SDK whitepaper over. Eng team lead will reach out tomorrow. Open for a 30min call next week?"
⊡

Auto-extract from any card

Forward a photo of a paper business card. M3 vision pulls out name, company, title, email — bot confirms and saves. Or forward a Telegram QR for instant save.

You: [forwards card photo]
Bot: Found: Jane Doe, Partner at Polychain Capital. Save this? [✓ Save] [✎ Edit]
⊕

Warm-intro matching

Ask "do I know anyone at Sequoia who can intro me" — bot searches your full network and ranks by warm-path strength.

You: who can intro me to someone at Sequoia
Bot: 2 paths. Strongest: @sequoia_k — met 5h ago, discussed L2. Weaker: @vc_partner — tagged 'investor', knows someone.
